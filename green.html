    • Cost Information

    With a change in energy consumption, it comes with a cost. Here we outline the money it may cost you to make the worthwhile switch to green energy.


    Solar Panels

  • The average solar panel installation cost in the USA is anywhere from $15,000-$24,000 (Numbers from ArchitecturalDigest.com)

    The per watt cost for energy produced by solar panels is generally $2.40-$3.60

    • Wind Turbine:

  • Cost of installation is anywhere from $10,000-$75,000 for residential turbines. (According to Kerry Thoubourn of EnergySage.com)
